## About

I am a Computer Science student at the University of Economics Ho Chi Minh City, focused on turning complex business and financial problems into reliable data models, dashboards, forecasts, and practical recommendations.

My technical foundation is built around SQL, Python, Power BI, dbt, data modeling, and modern analytics engineering. I enjoy building systems that move from raw data to decision-ready insight, whether that means a BI dashboard, an ETL pipeline, a data warehouse, a machine learning experiment, or an AI-assisted analytics workflow.

Portfolio: [tomidata.com](https://tomidata.com)

## Current Focus

- Business data analysis and decision-support dashboards
- Data engineering pipelines, SQL warehouses, and analytics marts
- Power BI semantic modeling and KPI systems
- Applied machine learning for computer vision and NLP research
- AI agents and Text-to-SQL workflows for faster data exploration
